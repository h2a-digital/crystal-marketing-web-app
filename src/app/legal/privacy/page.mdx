        app is backgrounded or a retry is needed).
      </div>

      <div className="mt-2">
        <strong>How long is it stored?</strong>
        <ul className="list-disc pl-5 mt-2 space-y-1">
          <li>
            <strong>Rock and crystal photos:</strong> We delete uploaded rock and crystal photos after the identification attempt
            completes. If deletion fails due to network or unexpected errors, we run automated cleanup to
            remove any remaining photo uploads within <strong>6 hours</strong>.
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div className="mt-2">
        <strong>Why this length?</strong> The <strong>6-hour</strong> window
        exist only as short reliability fallbacks (e.g., client disconnects, network errors, or retries) while
        minimizing retention of uploaded photos.
      </div>
    </div>

    <div>
      <div className="font-semibold text-gray-900">PostHog (Analytics)</div>

      <div className="mt-2">
